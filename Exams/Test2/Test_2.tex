Find the equations of directrices and coordinates of focus (or foci) of the following curves:
\begin{enumerate}

\item (5 points) 
Find the canonical equation of an ellipse (major axis is horizontal), if it is known that the eccentricity equals $0.5$ and the distance from its focus to the nearest vertex is 2.

\item (3 points)
Find the equations of the tangent and normal lines to the curve defined by the equation $x^2 - xy - y^2 + x + y = 0$ at the point with coordinates $(0.6,1.2)$ .
